== Installation ==
1. Navigate to Plugins > Add New.
2. Click the Upload Plugin button at the top of the screen.
3. Select the zip file from your local filesystem.
4. Click the Install Now button.

When installation is complete, you’ll see “Plugin installed successfully.” Click the Activate Plugin button at the bottom of the page.

5. Once WP Reactions plugin is activated, navigate to plugin dashboard to enter license key. 


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I control the pages/posts that I activate my emojis on? =
Yes. Simply choose manual mode when setting up your reactions and you will have the ability to activate at the page/post level while editing.

= Are your emojis licensed and legal to use? =
Yes. We only use the best licensed emojis available by JoyPixels and you are covered under our license to use on your website with our plugin [Learn more about our emojis](https://wpreactions.com/joypixels-animated-emojis).

= Are there any coding skills required? =
No. Not at all. We built WP Reactions for all users at all skill levels. Our step wizard will guide you through to make sure your reactions look great on your pages and posts.

= Are the emojis PNG or SVG files? =
Our emojis are SVG files only so you have the best quality and fastest loading reactions available.

= Can I turn off social sharing? =
Yes, we realize that you may have other plugins for that purpose already in place and we give you the option to disable when customizing your setup.

= Can I make up my own user counts? =
Yes, you can manage this option at the page and post level while editing. You will find our admin panel at the bottom and you can easily overwrite and manage your user counts. 

= Do I need to connect my site to your platform with an API? =
No. We do not require any connection. Simply install WP Reactions and you are good to go! 

= Are there analytics to understand how my users feel? =
Yes. On each page or post, you can access your statistics to see how your users are reacting to your content.

= How do I get more emojis? =
We offer 100 of the best premium Lottie animated emojis in our Pro version. In addition, we include 100 static SVG emojis. We recommend this upgrade for a more serious approach to user engagement.
